# Split-Pay Checkout

Split payments across multiple cards at Shopify checkout.

## What it does

Lets customers split a single purchase across 2-5 credit/debit cards. 
Perfect for couples, roommates, or anyone maximizing card rewards.

## Why it exists

Buy-now-pay-later is everywhere. But sometimes you just want to 
split a $500 couch with your roommate RIGHT NOW, not in 4 payments.

## Tech

Node.js • PostgreSQL • Stripe • Shopify App
